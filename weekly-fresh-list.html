🥬 Your Weekly Fresh List

The fresh items to grab locally each week — produce, eggs, dairy & fresh proteins.

Mom, your pantry staples & supplements arrive on their own (see your auto-delivery). This page is just the fresh things to pick up at the store each week. It follows your 6-week meal rotation, so each list matches that week's recipes — grab the matching list and you're set. Each week runs about $50–55. 💚

🛒 Grab these almost every week

  • Eggs
  • Greek (or soy) yogurt
  • Bananas
  • Apples
  • Baby spinach
  • Carrots
  • Onions & garlic
  • Avocados
