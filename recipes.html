🍽️ Mary's Easy Recipes

Quick, forgiving meals — protein & color in every one. Made to feel easy, not overwhelming.

Mom, these all reuse the same staples from your grocery list and auto-delivery, so nothing goes to waste. Cook once, eat twice — soups and chili freeze beautifully. The little tags show what's great for work 💼 and what's an easy home 🏠 meal. Don't aim for perfect — aim for fed and steady. 💛

🌅 Breakfasts

Overnight Oats & Berries💼 🏠

5 min the night before · ~15g protein
You need
  • ½ cup oats, ½ cup yogurt, ½ cup milk
  • 1 Tbsp ground flax, handful berries
  • Drizzle of honey
Make it: Stir in a jar, lid on, fridge overnight. Eat cold or warm. Make 3 jars at once for the week.

Veggie Egg Scramble🏠

5 min · ~20g protein
You need
  • 2 eggs, handful spinach
  • Cherry tomatoes or leftover veg
  • Olive oil, whole-grain toast
Make it: Wilt spinach & tomatoes 1 min, add beaten eggs, stir till set. Serve with toast.

Green Power Smoothie💼 🏠

3 min · ~20g protein
You need
  • 1 scoop protein or yogurt, 1 banana
  • Handful spinach, 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk (+ frozen berries optional)
Make it: Blend smooth, pour into a travel cup for the drive.

Greek Yogurt Parfait💼 🏠

2 min
You need
  • Greek or soy yogurt
  • Berries, spoon of nuts/granola
  • Drizzle of honey
Make it: Layer it all in a cup or jar. Perfect post-dance recovery.

Savory Oatmeal + Egg🏠

8 min · cozy & high-protein
You need
  • Oats cooked in broth (not milk)
  • 1 soft egg, handful spinach
  • Pepper, drizzle of olive oil
Make it: Cook oats in broth, top with egg & wilted spinach.

PB-Banana Toast + Egg💼 🏠

5 min
You need
  • Whole-grain toast + peanut butter
  • Banana slices
  • 1 boiled egg on the side
Make it: Toast, spread, top with banana. Egg adds staying power.

🥗 Lunches (work-friendly)

Mason-Jar Lentil Salad💼

10 min · keeps 4 days · ~18g protein
You need
  • 1 can lentils, chopped cucumber, tomato, pepper
  • Feta, olive oil + lemon
  • Greens on top
Make it: Dressing at the bottom, greens on top, shake at lunch. Make 2–3 jars at once.

Tuna or Chickpea Avocado Wrap💼 🏠

5 min · ~22g protein
You need
  • 1 can tuna or mashed chickpeas
  • ¼ avocado, lemon, salt, pepper
  • Whole-grain wrap, spinach, carrot
Make it: Mash, spread on wrap, add greens, roll. Foil-wrap the night before.

Loaded Sweet Potato💼 🏠

8 min microwave · ~15g protein
You need
  • 1 sweet potato, ½ can black beans
  • Salsa + yogurt (or guac)
  • Handful spinach
Make it: Microwave potato 6–8 min, pile on warm beans, greens, salsa, yogurt.

Hummus Veggie Plate💼

No cooking
You need
  • Hummus, carrots, peppers
  • Whole-grain crackers
  • Cheese stick or boiled egg
Make it: Arrange and enjoy — zero cook, easy desk lunch.

Grain Bowl💼 🏠

Built from leftovers
You need
  • Rice or quinoa
  • Beans or leftover chicken/salmon
  • Chopped veg + a drizzle of dressing
Make it: Pile leftovers over grains, dress, done.

Egg Salad Wrap💼 🏠

8 min · ~16g protein
You need
  • Boiled eggs
  • Spoon of yogurt/mayo + mustard
  • Wrap + greens
Make it: Mash eggs with yogurt & mustard, wrap with greens.

🍲 Dinners (light & easy)

Sheet-Pan Salmon & Veg🏠

25 min · one pan · ~30g protein
You need
  • Salmon fillet, broccoli, cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil, garlic, lemon
Make it: 400°F, toss veg + salmon in oil & lemon, bake 15–18 min. Leftovers = tomorrow's salad.

Cozy Lentil & Veggie Soup🏠 freezes

30 min · ~16g protein/bowl
You need
  • Lentils, onion, carrot, celery, garlic
  • Diced tomatoes, broth, spinach, cumin
Make it: Soften veg, add lentils + tomatoes + broth, simmer 20–25 min, stir in spinach. Freeze in single bowls.

Tofu or Shrimp Stir-Fry🏠

15 min · one pan · ~25g protein
You need
  • Firm tofu or shrimp
  • Bag of frozen stir-fry veg
  • Soy sauce, ginger, garlic, brown rice
Make it: Brown tofu/shrimp, add veg + sauce 5–6 min, serve over rice.

Bean & Veggie Chili🏠 freezes

30 min · ~18g protein/bowl
You need
  • Black + kidney beans, diced tomatoes
  • Onion, pepper, garlic, frozen corn
  • Cumin, chili powder
Make it: Soften veg, add beans, tomatoes, corn, spices; simmer 20 min. Top with yogurt + cheese.

Baked Chicken Thighs + Veg🏠

35 min · one pan · ~30g protein
You need
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • Sweet potato + broccoli
  • Olive oil, garlic, paprika
Make it: 400°F, toss together on a pan, bake 30–35 min (chicken 165°F). Extras = grain bowls.

Pasta with White Beans & Spinach🏠

20 min · ~16g protein
You need
  • Whole-grain pasta, 1 can white beans
  • Spinach, garlic, cherry tomatoes
  • Parmesan or feta
Make it: Boil pasta; warm oil + garlic + tomatoes, add beans & spinach to wilt, toss with pasta + cheese.

Black Bean & Sweet Potato Tacos🏠

20 min · fiber-rich
You need
  • Black beans, diced sweet potato
  • Whole-grain wraps
  • Salsa, avocado, cumin, lime
Make it: Roast/microwave sweet potato, warm beans with cumin, fill wraps, top & squeeze lime.

Veggie Fried Rice with Egg🏠

12 min · uses leftover rice · ~18g protein
You need
  • Cooked brown rice, 2 eggs
  • Frozen peas/carrots/veg
  • Garlic, low-sodium soy, oil
Make it: Scramble eggs & set aside, stir-fry veg + rice + soy, fold eggs back in.

Mediterranean Chickpea Couscous💼 🏠

15 min · ~16g protein
You need
  • Couscous or quinoa, 1 can chickpeas
  • Cucumber, tomato, feta
  • Olive oil, lemon, herbs
Make it: Pour boiling water over couscous, cover 5 min, fluff; stir in chickpeas, veg, feta, oil & lemon. Great warm or cold.

🍌 Snacks to always have on hand

Greek yogurt + berries · apple + peanut butter · handful of walnuts/almonds · hummus + carrots · hard-boiled eggs · edamame (microwave) · cheese stick + crackers · banana + ginger tea. Keep 3–4 of these stocked so "too tired to cook" still means "well fed." 💛
